Wildlife &
Nature Tourism

Zoos, nature parks, and eco-sanctuaries — Davao's wildlife destinations inspire wonder and awe in visitors of every age.

Tourism Category

Wildlife & Nature Tourism
in Davao

Nature Parks Interactive Wildlife Botanical Gardens Conservation Focus

Davao is a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts, offering a wide array of zoos, nature parks, and eco-tourism attractions. Known for its rich biodiversity and strong conservation culture, the city and its surroundings host wildlife sanctuaries, adventure parks, and interactive animal experiences unlike anywhere else in the Philippines.

Whether you visit with family, friends, or on a solo adventure, Davao's wildlife destinations will give you an unforgettable encounter with the natural world — from crocodiles and exotic birds to rare butterflies and breathtaking highland forest landscapes.

Crocodile Park

Up-close encounters with crocodiles, exotic birds, and snakes — plus thrilling feeding shows at Davao's premier wildlife attraction.

Butterfly House

A magical sanctuary of rare and endemic butterflies and insects set within beautiful lush tropical gardens.

Malagos Garden

Cacao farm tours, bird shows, butterfly gardens, and the famous bean-to-bar chocolate experience — all in one destination.

Eden Nature Park

A 100-hectare highland retreat offering hiking trails, bird watching, a mini-zoo, and stunning panoramic views.

Davao Crocodile Park

The Ultimate
Wildlife Experience

The Davao Crocodile Park stands as one of the city's premier wildlife attractions, offering visitors an up-close encounter with a diverse array of remarkable creatures. At its heart are impressive collections of crocodiles — from tiny hatchlings to fully grown adults — housed in carefully designed, educational enclosures.

Beyond the reptiles, the park features a vibrant aviary with exotic birds, a fascinating collection of snakes and other reptiles, and engaging educational tours that delve into the biology and conservation of these animals. The undeniable highlight for many visitors is the thrilling crocodile feeding show.

The Crocodile Park is both an entertainment venue and a conservation center — working passionately to educate visitors about the importance of protecting these often-misunderstood animals and the ecosystems that depend on them.

Davao Butterfly House

A Magical Insect
Sanctuary

The Davao Butterfly House is an enchanting attraction that allows visitors to immerse themselves in a world of vibrant, fluttering butterflies amidst lush, tropical gardens. Beyond the kaleidoscope of butterfly species, the house also showcases rare and exotic insects, offering a broader perspective on the region's extraordinary entomological diversity.

The experience is designed to be highly interactive and educational — providing valuable insights into the life cycles of various butterfly species, their unique physical characteristics, and their crucial ecological roles as pollinators and indicators of environmental health.

The Butterfly House is a perfect destination for families with children, school groups, and anyone who wants to connect with the delicate, colorful world of Mindanao's incredible insect life in a safe and beautiful garden setting.

Malagos Garden Resort

Birds, Butterflies,
and Chocolate

More than just a wildlife sanctuary, Malagos Garden Resort is a comprehensive cultural and eco-tourism destination that has garnered wide acclaim for its captivating interactive bird shows, beautiful butterfly gardens, and world-renowned cacao farm that produces some of the Philippines' finest artisan chocolate.

A significant highlight of any visit is the resort's bean-to-bar chocolate-making experience — guiding visitors step-by-step through the complete journey from cacao pod cultivation to the final creation of exquisite premium chocolate products that have earned international recognition.

This unique and seamless combination of wildlife, culture, and artisan food production makes Malagos one of Davao's most beloved and frequently visited destinations — a place where every visit reveals something new and wonderful.

Eden Nature Park

A Highland Wildlife
Retreat

Spanning an expansive 100 hectares, Eden Nature Park is a breathtaking highland mountain resort offering a rich tapestry of natural wonders and outdoor adventure activities. Visitors are welcomed into lush, verdant forests where opportunities for wildlife encounters, bird watching, and nature photography abound at every turn.

The park provides well-maintained hiking trails, dedicated bird-watching areas, and stunning panoramic viewpoints overlooking the broader Davao region. Meticulously curated botanical gardens and a charming mini-zoo house a variety of local and exotic animals — making Eden a truly comprehensive highland nature experience.

Whether you are searching for tranquility amid nature, an active outdoor adventure, or a meaningful encounter with Davao's extraordinary biodiversity, Eden Nature Park delivers an unforgettable highland experience for every type of visitor.
