Vibrant coral gardens, underwater caves, and crystal-clear waters — Davao del Norte's marine world awaits just beneath the surface.

Snorkeling & Diving
in Davao del Norte

Crystal Clear Waters World-Class Dive Sites Samal Island & Gulf Marine Conservation

Diving into the clear, turquoise waters of Davao del Norte opens a gateway to a vibrant underwater world. From shallow, sun-dappled reefs teeming with colorful fish to expansive coral gardens and challenging underwater cave systems, the marine landscapes here provide breathtaking experiences for snorkelers and divers of all skill levels.

Samal Island's dive sites — including the legendary Ligid Caves — are recognized as some of the finest in the Philippines, attracting underwater explorers from across the region and around the world who come in search of an unforgettable dive experience.

Ligid Caves

World-Class Underwater
Cave Diving

Ligid Caves are a unique and extraordinary underwater dive site located on Samal Island, featuring a system of caverns and tunnels suitable for advanced divers at a maximum depth of 40 meters. The site is recognized as one of the finest cave dive sites in the entire Philippines.

Divers can explore fascinating rock formations and swim-throughs while encountering remarkable marine life — schools of fish, black corals, soft corals, sponge crabs, morays, and occasionally sharks. The combination of geological wonder and extraordinary marine biodiversity makes Ligid Caves a truly world-class experience.

Advanced diving certification is recommended for Ligid Caves — but for those with the skills and experience, it represents one of the most rewarding underwater adventures available anywhere in Southeast Asia.

Isla Reta Beach Resort

Serene Island Escape
on Talicud

Isla Reta Beach Resort, nestled on Talicud Island within the Island Garden City of Samal, offers an affordable and tranquil escape ideal for families and snorkeling enthusiasts. The resort is celebrated for its pristine white-sand beach meeting crystal-clear, calm waters that are perfect for casual snorkeling and leisurely swimming.

The atmosphere at Isla Reta is one of serene natural beauty and laid-back comfort — a perfect destination for those who want to snorkel, swim, and relax in a genuine island environment without the crowds and premium prices of larger resort developments.

The resort is also a great base for exploring the broader waters around Talicud Island, where shallow coral gardens and abundant fish life reward snorkelers with vibrant underwater vistas just meters from the shoreline.

Carabao Dive Resort

Professional Diving
with a Conservation Heart

Carabao Dive Center is operated by professional scuba divers from diverse backgrounds, all united by a shared passion for the ocean and a deep commitment to marine environmental conservation. The center's vision centers on becoming the market leader in scuba diving in this part of the Philippines.

The center provides value-added scuba diving services and premium dive gear while remaining strong advocates for the marine ecosystems they introduce their guests to. Sustainability, safety, and conservation are core values embedded in every dive experience they offer.

Whether you are a first-time diver or a seasoned underwater explorer, Carabao Dive Center offers a professional, welcoming environment that will make your Samal Island diving experience truly memorable.

Wind and Wave Davao

Premier Aqua Sports
Since 1993

Wind and Wave Davao, a premier aqua sports and diving center, has been a cornerstone of aquatic adventure since its establishment in mid-1993. Located at the Fiesta Airways Hangar within the General Aviation area of the Sasa Old Airport in Davao City, it serves as a central hub for the region's diving community.

Operating as a subsidiary of AYP Holdings Inc., Wind and Wave Davao is accredited by the Philippine Commission on Sports Scuba Diving, the Department of Tourism (DOT), and PADI IRRA — ensuring world-class standards of safety, professionalism, and service quality for every guest who walks through its doors.

With over three decades of experience, Wind and Wave Davao is an institution in Mindanao's diving scene — a trusted name for dive education, equipment, guided dives, and everything else the underwater adventurer needs.
