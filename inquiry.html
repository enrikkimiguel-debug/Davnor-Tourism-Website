Why Visit Davao del Norte?

Year-Round DestinationOutside the typhoon belt — enjoy beautiful weather any time of year.

World-Class BeachesSamal Island's white sand shores and clear waters are among the finest in Southeast Asia.

Untouched NatureFrom mountain forests to river gorges — incredible wilderness awaits explorers.

Rich Indigenous CultureAuthentic Lumad and Manobo traditions, festivals, and crafts you won't find anywhere else.

Tropical Food ParadiseFresh durian, rambutan, exotic fruits, and Mindanaoan flavors straight from the source.
